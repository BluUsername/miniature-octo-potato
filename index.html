  1. Click Start Quiz to begin.

  4. Click Next to move to the next question, or use Enter.
  5. You can go back using the Back button or Backspace to change an answer.
  6. Toggle between Light/Dark mode using the theme button in the header.
  7. At the end, you'll see your score and explanations.
